Privacy Policy for Times Table Game

Last updated: October 25, 2025

Overview

This app does not collect, store, or transmit any personal data to external servers. Your privacy is fully protected.

Speech Recognition & Microphone Access

  • Speech recognition is processed entirely on your device using Apple's built-in services
  • No audio recordings are saved or transmitted anywhere
  • Microphone access is only active during gameplay when you choose Audio mode
  • You can revoke microphone permissions at any time in your device Settings

Data Storage

  • All game scores and preferences are stored locally on your device only
  • No data is sent to external servers or cloud services
  • No user accounts, registration, or authentication required
  • No personally identifiable information is collected

Third-Party Services

This app does not use any third-party analytics, advertising, or tracking services.

Children's Privacy

This app is safe for children. We do not collect any data from users of any age.

Changes to This Policy

If we make changes to this privacy policy, we will update the "Last updated" date at the top of this page.
