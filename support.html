Frequently Asked Questions

How do I enable Audio mode?

When you select Audio mode, the app will ask for microphone and speech recognition permissions. If you denied these previously, go to Settings → Privacy & Security → Microphone (and Speech Recognition) and enable access for Times Table Game.

The app isn't recognizing my voice

  • Make sure you're in a quiet environment
  • Speak clearly and at a normal pace
  • Wait for the "Listening..." indicator before speaking
  • Say just the number (e.g., "twelve" or "12")

How do I reset my score?

The cumulative score tracks your progress across sessions. Currently, you'll need to delete and reinstall the app to reset it.

What data does the app collect?

None! All data stays on your device. See our Privacy Policy for details.

Troubleshooting

Audio mode not working

  1. Check that microphone permissions are enabled in Settings
  2. Check that speech recognition permissions are enabled
  3. Try force-closing and reopening the app
  4. Restart your device

App crashes or freezes

  • Make sure you're running the latest version of iOS
  • Force-close the app and reopen it
  • Restart your device
